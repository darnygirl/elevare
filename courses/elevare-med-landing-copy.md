## Section 10 — FAQ

**Q: How is this different from Kaplan OET or E2 Language?**
A: Kaplan and E2 are self-paced courses — videos + exercises, no real coach in your corner. Elevare Med is a real 1:1 coach relationship + AI practice between sessions. We cost less than Kaplan and give you actual human time.

**Q: Do I need to live in Chiang Mai / be in-country?**
A: No. Elevare Med is fully remote. Students join from Philippines, India, Nigeria, Kenya, UAE, Singapore, Thailand. All sessions are Zoom-based.

**Q: What if I fail my OET despite finishing the 12 weeks?**
A: Your programme extends free until you hit Grade B on all 4 papers. No catch, no extra charges. See the guarantee above.

**Q: Can I pay monthly?**
A: Yes — $699/month, 12-week commitment. Or $1,999 upfront saves you ~15%.

**Q: Do I need to already be at B1 English?**
A: Yes — Elevare Med assumes conversational fluency. Your assessment call will confirm. If you're below B1, we'll recommend starting with Elevare Speak ($199/mo) and moving to Med once you're ready.

**Q: What's the time commitment?**
A: One 60-min coach session weekly + 30 minutes daily AI practice + self-study of our materials. Realistic for working nurses on shift.

**Q: What if I have to skip a week for a shift emergency?**
A: One free catch-up session per 12-week programme, no questions asked. Beyond that we'll work around your rota.

**Q: Who built this course?**
A: Elevare's founder has 10+ years teaching English internationally, and the Med curriculum was developed with input from current OET-certified examiners and NHS-registered nurses.

**Q: How do I start?**
A: Book your free 10-minute assessment. You'll get a personalized 3-week starting plan within 24 hours regardless of whether you enrol.
