# Elevare Pro — Course Outline (Skeleton)

> Business English for working professionals. Conforms to `implementation-methodology.md` 5-stage loop. Skeleton only — expand to full curriculum as the product moves toward launch.

---

## Positioning + ICP

**Primary ICP:** Chiang Mai expats + SEA professionals who need English to:
- Close deals (sales, business development)
- Lead teams (management, standups, 1:1s)
- Pitch (investors, clients, conferences)
- Present (slide decks, keynotes)
- Network (conferences, dinners, industry events)

**Secondary ICP (later):** remote workers at English-native companies needing to keep up in fast-moving chat + calls.

**Pricing:** $449/mo (raised from $349 per Section 18 pricing update). 60-min weekly 1:1 + 60-min weekly small-group (3-max) + Elevare AI scenario-focused practice.

---

## Stage 1 — Intake

- 10-min assessment call (longer than standard, Pro buyers expect consultation)
- Business context diagnostic: industry, role, English usage frequency, top 3 scenarios they're avoiding
- Tier-match: Pro entry requires B1+ English floor. Below that → Speak first.

---

## Stage 2 — Onboard

- Welcome pack + Pro methodology PDF
- Personalised scenario list (their top 5 avoided moments become curriculum thread)
- First 1:1 within 72 hrs with senior coach (Pro gets higher-tier tutors)

---

## Stage 3 — Deliver (monthly rolling, 4-week themes)

**Theme rotation (each student customizes order based on urgency):**

| Week | Theme | Core practice |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | High-stakes meetings | Leading, interjecting, disagreeing, recovering from mistakes |
| 2 | Presentations + pitches | Slide narrative, Q&A handling, storytelling frameworks |
| 3 | Negotiation + difficult conversations | Price talk, pushback, "no" without losing relationship |
| 4 | Written communication | Email tone, Slack/Teams register, professional writing |

**Custom track:** each student's top 3 avoided scenarios rotate into role-plays throughout the month.

**Small-group format (3 students):** each week simulates one realistic cross-company meeting. Students role-play different functions.

---

## Stage 4 — Measure

- Weekly coach rubric (5-skill scoring)
- Monthly "real-world deployment" check: did the student use the skill in an actual work situation? What happened? Iteration loop.

---

## Stage 5 — Graduate or Renew

- Auto-renew — Pro is typically multi-month, 4-6 month retention targets
- Upsell: Pro → Pro Annual (20% off, lock-in), Pro + Med combo for medical professionals
- Referral incentive: $100 credit for each new Pro student (high-value referrals)
