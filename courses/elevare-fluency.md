# Elevare Fluency — Course Outline (Skeleton)

> 12-week structured fluency intensive. **Rebranded from Flex Nexus** per Debby's 2026-04-17 decision. Conforms to `implementation-methodology.md` 5-stage loop. Skeleton only — full curriculum TBD before launch.

---

## Positioning + ICP

**ICP:** committed learners who want a finite 12-week intensive to lock in fluency gains. Often the "I'm getting married in 6 months" / "I have a job interview next quarter" / "my visa requires this" buyer with real deadline pressure.

**Pricing:** $497 upfront for 12-week programme, OR $199 × 3 monthly. Test raising to $697 in Month 3 once testimonials land.

**Format:** 60-min weekly group call (6-max cohort) + 30-min weekly 1:1 + Elevare AI daily practice + structured curriculum. Graduation certificate + LinkedIn badge at Week 12.

---

## Stage 1 — Intake

- Standard 5-min assessment
- Fluency-gap diagnostic: 10-min speaking + 5-min writing sample
- Commitment conversation: "Can you put in 30 min/day for 12 weeks? If not, this isn't the right tier."

---

## Stage 2 — Onboard

- Welcome pack + programme roadmap PDF (week-by-week preview)
- Cohort introduction — other 5 students, their goals
- First 1:1 books for Day 2; first group call for Day 3
- AI provisioned with Fluency scenario set

---

## Stage 3 — Deliver (12 weeks)

**Skeleton arc:**

| Weeks | Phase | Core focus |
|---|---|---|
| 1-2 | Baseline + fluency floor | Clearing hesitation, basic structures automatic |
| 3-4 | Range + confidence | Topic range, opinion-giving, argument frames |
| 5-6 | Real-world deployment | Scenarios from student's actual life (work/study/social) |
| 7-8 | Precision + accuracy | Grammar edges that still slip, register-matching |
| 9-10 | Complex communication | Nuance, humour, storytelling, negotiation |
| 11 | Integration | Weak-spot pass, final prep |
| 12 | Graduation | Final assessment + celebration + LinkedIn badge + testimonial capture |

**Rise layer intensified** through weeks 7-12 as confidence gaps show up at intermediate levels.

---

## Stage 4 — Measure

- Week 4: baseline + milestone 1 assessment (public in cohort — peer accountability)
- Week 8: milestone 2
- Week 12: final assessment + graduation decision

**Graduation criteria:** measurable progress on student's stated Week-1 goal (individualized — not a one-size rubric).

---

## Stage 5 — Graduate or Renew

- Graduation session = marketing gold (on-camera, cohort group photo, LinkedIn post)
- Upsell: Fluency → Pro (career focus) / Med (medical) / Speak (continued monthly practice)
- Alumni community: WhatsApp group for graduates — zero-cost retention asset
