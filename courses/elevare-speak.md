# Elevare Speak — Course Outline (Skeleton)

> Coach-led group cohort for conversation fluency. Conforms to `implementation-methodology.md` 5-stage loop. Skeleton only — expand to full per-week curriculum as the product moves toward launch.

---

## Positioning + ICP

**ICP:** working adults globally who want conversation fluency — general English, not specialized. Mid-tier in the Elevare funnel. Middle between Elevare AI (price-sensitive) and Pro/Med (niche specialized).

**Pricing:** $199/mo (raised from $149 per Section 18 pricing update). 90-min group call weekly + 15-min bi-weekly 1:1 + Elevare AI between sessions.

**Cohort:** 6 students max. New cohort starts every 4 weeks. Students can join mid-cycle with a catch-up session.

---

## Stage 1 — Intake

- 5-min assessment call (standard)
- CEFR estimate — Speak sits well for A2-B2 learners
- Cohort-match: assign to next starting cohort based on level + availability

---

## Stage 2 — Onboard

- Welcome pack + cohort intro video (who else is in your cohort)
- Book first group session
- Pre-session prep (10 min watchlist / vocab priming)

---

## Stage 3 — Deliver (monthly rolling, ~4 weeks per theme)

**Theme rotation (repeats every 4-6 months so returning students don't repeat):**

| Week | Theme | Core practice |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Small talk + introductions | 6 scenarios, pair practice |
| 2 | Telling stories in English | Past tense fluency, hesitation recovery |
| 3 | Opinions + disagreement politely | Argument frames, hedging language |
| 4 | Dealing with unexpected situations | Improv, "what if" scenarios |

**Structure repeats with different topics in month 2, 3, 4, etc.**

---

## Stage 4 — Measure

- Weekly coach scoring (confidence + fluency + accuracy)
- Student monthly self-rating
- Day 30 progress check-in call (free 15-min)

---

## Stage 5 — Graduate or Renew

- Auto-renew monthly
- Upsell path: Speak → Pro (if student is working professional) or Speak → Fluency (if committed learner wanting structured program)

---
