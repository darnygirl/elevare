# How to Set Up Calendly for Elevare

## Step 1: Log In to Calendly

1. Go to **https://calendly.com/elevareworkplace**
2. Log in with your credentials

---

## Step 2: Create Your First Event Type

**Event types** are the types of sessions you offer. Let's create them:

### Event 1: Free Consultation (30 minutes)

1. Click **"Event Types"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the green **"Create"** button
3. Fill in the details:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Event Name** | `Free Consultation` |
| **Description** | `Get to know us and explore how we can help you achieve your language and personal development goals.` |
| **Duration** | `30 minutes` |
| **Location** | `Video Call` (select Jitsi or Zoom - or leave as Video) |
| **Color** | `#c9a227` (gold) |

4. Click **"Event Type"** to save
5. Copy the link - it will look like: `calendly.com/elevareworkplace/free-consultation`

---

### Event 2: 1-on-1 Coaching (60 minutes)

1. Click **"Create"** again
2. Fill in:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Event Name** | `1-on-1 Coaching Session` |
| **Description** | `Personalized coaching session tailored to your specific needs and learning pace.` |
| **Duration** | `60 minutes` |
| **Location** | `Video Call` |
| **Color** | `#c9a227` (gold) |

3. Click **"Event Type"** to save

---

### Event 3: Group Workshop (90 minutes)

1. Click **"Create"** again
2. Fill in:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Event Name** | `Group Workshop` |
| **Description** | `Join a small group session with others at your level. Learn from peers and collaborate.` |
| **Duration** | `90 minutes` |
| **Location** | `Video Call` |
| **Color** | `#c9a227` (gold) |

3. Click **"Event Type"** to save

---

## Step 3: Set Your Availability

1. Go to **"Availability"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Edit"** on your default schedule
3. Set your available hours:
   - **Monday - Friday:** 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (your timezone)
4. Make sure you're available on days you want to teach
5. Click **"Save"**

---

## Step 4: Set Up Email Notifications

So you get notified when someone books:

1. Go to **"Notifications"** in the left sidebar
2. Under **"Email Notifications"**, make sure these are ON:

| Notification | When | Toggle |
|--------------|-------|--------|
| **Confirmation email to invitee** | Immediately | ✅ ON |
| **Reminder to invitee** | 24 hours before | ✅ ON |
| **Notify me** | When someone books | ✅ ON |

3. Click **"Save"**

---

## Step 5: Connect Video Conferencing (Optional)

Calendly can automatically add video links to bookings:

### Option A: Jitsi (Free - Recommended)

1. Go to **"Video Conferencing"** in settings
2. Look for **"Jitsi"** or **"Add video conferencing"**
3. Enable it

### Option B: Zoom

1. Go to **"Video Conferencing"**
2. Connect your Zoom account
3. Select Zoom as your default for all events

### Option C: Google Meet

1. Go to **"Video Conferencing"**
2. Connect your Google account
3. Select Google Meet as your default

---

## Troubleshooting

### "Calendly is not available"
- Make sure you completed your Calendly profile
- Verify your email address

### Booking button not working
- Make sure the event type URL matches your website
- Check that the event is "Active"

### No confirmation email sent
- Check your spam folder
- Verify notification settings in Calendly

---

## What Happens When Someone Books

1. Student clicks your booking link
2. Selects a time that works for them
3. Enters their name and email
4. Gets a confirmation email with the video link
5. You get an email notification with the student's details
6. The video call happens via Jitsi/Zoom
