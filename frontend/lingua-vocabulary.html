Daily Life

Words for everyday conversations
Schedule
/ˈʃedjuːl/
A plan for carrying out a process
"I check my schedule every morning."
Appointments
/əˈpɔɪntmənts/
Arranged meetings at set times
"I have an appointment at 3pm."
Errands
/ˈerəndz/
Short trips for practical purposes
"I need to run some errands."
Household
/ˈhaʊshəʊld/
People living together as a unit
"Our household has four people."
Routine
/ruːˈtiːn/
A regular way of doing things
"I follow a morning routine."
Chores
/ˈtʃɔːrz/
Regular domestic tasks
"I do household chores on weekends."
