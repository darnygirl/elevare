FAQ

How is this different from ChatGPT?

Elevare AI is purpose-built for language practice. It's trained on our methodology (psychology-informed, Rise layer, coach-augmenting), hosts on WhatsApp (not a web tab), and integrates with Elevare coach-led tiers. ChatGPT is a general tool; Elevare AI is a practice companion with a clear learning arc.

Will it replace my human coach?

No — it augments coaches, never replaces. The between-session recap mode literally requires coach input. AI is the gym; the coach is the trainer.

What level does Elevare AI work for?

A2 to B2. Below A2, the AI conversations get too hard. Above B2, you'll outgrow the scenarios in a few weeks and should move to Pro or Fluency. We'll tell you when we see you ready.

Is it voice or text?

Text first (launch version). Voice mode ships in Month 2-3, so all AI tier members get it automatically when ready. Voice uses your account's available minutes per WhatsApp.

What's in the 30+ scenarios?

Daily life (shopping, travel, meetings, small talk), work (presentations, negotiations, interviews), and learning (tutoring role-play, test prep practice). New scenarios added weekly.

Can I cancel?

Yes, anytime. Monthly subscription, no commitment beyond the current month.

Do I need to be on WhatsApp?

Yes — Elevare AI is WhatsApp-native. If you don't use WhatsApp, let us know at signup and we'll work with you.
