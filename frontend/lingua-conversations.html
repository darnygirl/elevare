Meeting Someone New

Introducing yourself and making small talk
Person A
"Hi there! My name is Sarah. Nice to meet you!"
打招呼並自我介紹
Person B
"Hello, Sarah. It's nice to meet you too. I'm Michael."
礼貌地回應自我介紹
Person A
"Where are you from, Michael?"
詢問對方來自哪裡
Person B
"I'm from Toronto, Canada. And you? Where are you from?"
回答並反問
Person A
"I'm from London. It's great to meet someone from North America!"
分享並表達友好

At a Coffee Shop

Ordering food and drinks
Barista
"Good morning! Welcome to Blue Sky Cafe. What can I get for you today?"
服務員招呼
Customer
"Good morning! I'd like a medium latte, please."
點飲料
Barista
"Would you like that hot or iced?"
服務員詢問溫度
Customer
"Hot, please. Also, do you have any pastries today?"
回答並追加詢問
Barista
"Yes! We have fresh croissants and blueberry muffins."
服務員介紹糕點
Customer
"Perfect! I'll have a croissant too. That's everything, thank you!"
完成點單

At the Office

Asking for help and scheduling meetings
Colleague
"Excuse me, do you have a moment? I need some help with this project."
礼貌地打斷並請求幫助
You
"Of course! I'd be happy to help. What seems to be the problem?"
積極回應
Colleague
"I'm having trouble with the formatting. Would you be able to take a look?"
說明具體問題
You
"Sure, let me see... Ah, I see the issue. Click on the Layout tab and choose the centered option."
提供解決方案
Colleague
"Oh, that's perfect! Thank you so much for your help!"
表達感謝
You
"No problem at all! Let me know if you need anything else. I'm free tomorrow if you want to schedule a meeting to discuss this further."
主動提供後續幫助

Asking for Directions

Getting around in a new place
Tourist
"Excuse me, could you help me? I'm looking for the train station."
禮貌地詢問方向
Local
"Of course! The train station is about a ten-minute walk from here."
確認並給出距離
Tourist
"Great, thanks! Could you tell me how to get there?"
詢問具體路線
Local
"Sure! Go straight ahead on this street until you reach the traffic lights. Then turn left. The station is on your right, next to the big library."
提供詳細指示
Tourist
"I understand. Straight to the lights, then left. Thank you so much for your help!"
重複確認並感謝
Local
"You're welcome! Have a great day and good luck!"
友好地結束對話
