Your free trial includes a 30-minute consultation where we assess your goals, a personalized learning path recommendation, and access to our free 5-day confidence course. No credit card required.
Can I switch programs after starting? +
Absolutely. Many students start in one program and realize they want to focus on another, or discover they need the Complete Bundle. We make switching easy — just let your coach know.
How do payments work? +
We accept credit cards (Stripe), Whop, and LINE Pay. Monthly programs bill on the same day each month. Flex Nexus can be paid upfront or in 3 installments.

Cancel anytime with 24 hours notice. No cancellation fees, no hassle. Monthly programs have no long-term commitment. We want you to continue because you're seeing results, not because you're locked in.

All sessions can be recorded (with your permission) for review. We also provide session summaries and action items after each call. Many students find reviewing recordings accelerates their progress.

Most students notice improvement within the first week. By day 30, 95% of our students report measurable progress. Our coaches track specific metrics so you can see your growth objectively.
