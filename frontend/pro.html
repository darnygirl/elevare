Each theme repeats monthly with harder variants. Your specific upcoming scenarios — real meetings, real pitches, real emails — become the curriculum.

Week 1 — High-stakes meetings

Lead without ceding control. Interject politely when someone else dominates. Disagree firmly without damaging the relationship.

  • PREP framework for opinions
  • 15-phrase interjection bank
  • Disagreement escalator (soft to firm)
  • Mid-meeting recovery language

Week 2 — Presentations & pitches

Structure narrative arcs that land. Handle Q&A without defensive postures. Close with a specific ask, not vague next steps.

  • MOTH storytelling framework
  • SCQA for openings
  • Q&A bridge language
  • Close-with-ask patterns

Week 3 — Negotiation & difficult conversations

Anchor pricing without apology. Deliver bad news with dignity for both parties. Say "no" as a complete sentence when needed.

  • BATNA positioning
  • Mirror + label techniques
  • Bad news sandwich
  • Silence as strategy

Week 4 — Written communication

Match tone to context — email, Slack, LinkedIn, client-facing docs. Avoid over-politeness that undermines authority.

  • Register matrix for 6 contexts
  • BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)
  • Polite ≠ effective
  • Written negotiation

FAQ

How is Pro different from Speak?

Speak is cohort-based general fluency ($199). Pro is business-specific, 1:1 heavy, with senior coaches ($449). Pro assumes B1+ and focuses on work scenarios — meetings, pitches, negotiation, written comms. Speak is broader — travel, social, everyday conversation.

Do I need to be B1+?

Yes. Pro assumes conversational fluency. Your assessment call confirms. Below B1 — start with Speak.

What's the 3-month minimum?

Meaningful progress on business-English precision takes at least 12 weeks. We don't want to sell you one month and disappoint you. Typical retention is 4-8 months.

Is there an industry-specific variant?

Yes — tech, finance, medical leadership, real estate, consulting. When 3+ students in an industry enrol, the scenario library gets industry-specific variants. Medical professionals often combine Pro + Med.

Can my employer reimburse this?

Most companies with professional development budgets cover Pro. We provide invoices in your name. Reach out if you need a specific format.

Can I cancel?

After the 3-month minimum, yes — anytime. We don't lock you in beyond that.
