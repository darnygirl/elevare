The 4-week rotating curriculum

Master the 4 conversation moves that matter

Every 4 weeks we cycle these themes with new scenarios and deeper challenges. Stay as long as you want — every cycle compounds.

WEEK 1

Small talk + introductions

Hold a 5-min conversation without awkward silence. Active listening phrases. Opening + closing with grace.

WEEK 2

Telling stories

Past tense fluency under pressure. Narrative arc. Emotional language beyond "good" and "bad." Recover when you lose the thread.

WEEK 3

Opinions + polite disagreement

Express strong views without aggression. Disagreement ladder from soft to firm. Hedging + clarification phrases.

WEEK 4

Improv + recovery

Keep speaking when you don't know the word. Recover from misunderstandings. Handle unexpected questions without freezing.

Questions answered

FAQ

Who is Speak for?

Working adults at A2-B2 level who want conversation fluency for work, travel, social life, or family. Not beginners (A0-A1 need a dedicated beginner track). Not test prep (use Fluency or Med for that).

How is this different from Elevare AI ($29)?

Elevare AI is AI-only practice. Speak adds a real human coach + a cohort of peers + personalized drills. The human-in-loop is what moves you out of the intermediate plateau.

Can I join mid-cycle?

Yes — we run a 30-min catch-up session so you're not behind. No prorating needed.

What if my cohort doesn't feel right?

Flag us in the first 2 sessions and we'll move you to a different cohort at no extra cost. Good cohorts are critical to the product.

Do I need to be on camera?

Encouraged but not required. Many students start voice-only for the first few sessions as they build confidence. The Rise methodology meets you where you are.

What happens after 4 weeks?

The curriculum repeats with new, harder variants. Many students stay 6-12+ months as conversation maintenance. Natural graduation is when you stop thinking about frameworks and just talk.

Is there a commitment?

Monthly subscription, cancel anytime. That said, most fluency gains show in month 2, not month 1 — give it a real shot.
