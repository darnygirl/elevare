# Recording Pack — Tomorrow

> 4 tutors × (3 photos + 1× 30-sec clip) · ~30 min per tutor with prep · anchored to current 3-tier taxonomy (General / Pro / Med)

---

## Before rolling (shared)

- **Lighting**: face a window, not a light. Morning or late afternoon. Never overhead fluorescent.
- **Sound**: quiet room, phone or laptop mic ~30 cm from mouth. Kill fan / AC for the take.
- **Framing**: head + shoulders. Eyes in top 1/3 of frame. Horizontal for clip, square for photos.
- **Wear**: plain top, no logos, no busy patterns. Same outfit across photos + clip so they pair.
- **Backup**: record 2 takes each. Second take always sounds less nervous.

## 30-sec clip — the arc

Every clip follows the same 4-beat structure. 30 sec ≈ 75 words.

1. **Hook** (5 s) — one sentence naming the student's pain
2. **Promise** (8 s) — what you do + why it works
3. **Proof** (10 s) — one concrete before/after or student beat
4. **CTA** (7 s) — "Come work with me at Elevare [tier]" + eye contact

**Do not say** — these are dead names: Lingua · Rise · Flex Nexus · Elevare AI · Elevare Speak · Elevare Fluency.

**Do say** — current taxonomy: Elevare General ($99/mo) · Elevare Pro ($299/mo) · Elevare Med ($699/mo).
