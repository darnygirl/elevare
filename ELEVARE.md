# Elevare Project Context

## Mission
Elevare is a language learning and personal development coaching platform with three pillars: Lingua (language fluency), Rise (confidence building), and Flex Nexus (professional communication). Our mission is to help people overcome communication barriers through mental-health-informed coaching that addresses the psychology behind speaking, not just the mechanics.
