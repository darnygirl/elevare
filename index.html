FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Elevare programs and services

How do I book a session?

Fill out our booking form here or message us directly on WhatsApp. We'll confirm your session within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via Skrill. After booking, we'll send you a secure payment link. Bank transfers can be arranged for larger packages.

Are the sessions conducted online?

Yes! All sessions are conducted online via video call. You'll receive a link to join before your scheduled session.

What's included in the free consultation?

Your free 30-minute consultation includes discussing your goals, exploring program options, and creating a personalized learning path. No commitment required.

Can I become a tutor with Elevare?

Absolutely! We welcome tutors in language, life coaching, and professional development. Apply here to join our network and access training, resources, and students worldwide.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Contact us via WhatsApp or email as soon as possible, and we'll find a convenient alternative time for you.

How long until I see results?

Many students report noticeable improvement within 30 days. The timeline varies by program and individual commitment. Our structured approach ensures steady, measurable progress.

Do you offer group sessions?

Yes! We offer group workshops at reduced rates (up to 8 participants). Perfect for colleagues, friends, or classmates learning together. Book a group session to get started.
